The func.py file includes the necessary data wrangling functions.

Plot_script.py , plot the country happiness scores against their FXRATE : that is how much has the value of the currency has changed between two dates ( beggining and end of year 2019.)

api_data.py : downloads the neceessary data from echangeratesapi.com .

scraper.py , scrapes the country and happiness score from  wikipedia .

scrape_currency.py : is a script which downloads the country and the ISO currency codes from www.countries-ofthe-world.
